HabitU (“the App”) is a habit tracking application designed to help users build and maintain positive habits. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

By using HabitU, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

HabitU allows you to create and manage habits. All habit-related data you enter into the App, such as habit names, schedules, and completion history, is stored locally on your device.

We do not require account registration and do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Precise location

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect limited, non-personal information automatically, including:

  • App usage data
  • Crash reports
  • Device information (such as operating system version)

This information is used solely to improve app stability, performance, and user experience.

1.3 Purchase and Subscription Information

HabitU offers optional in-app purchases and subscriptions. Purchase-related information is processed by third-party services and app stores. We do not have access to your full payment details.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain core app functionality
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Manage subscriptions and purchase entitlements

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

HabitU uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • RevenueCat – for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Google Play Store – for Android purchases and distribution
  • Apple App Store – for iOS purchases and distribution

Other third-party libraries used in the App are limited to user interface, visualization, and app functionality purposes and do not collect or transmit personal data.

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Habit data is stored locally on your device.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your information.
  • No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be removed from your device.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data directly within the App
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the App
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through your app store account

HabitU does not maintain user accounts or store personal data on external servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

HabitU does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
