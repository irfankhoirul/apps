Privacy Policy for Markk Habit Tracker

Last updated: Feb 27, 2026

Markk Habit Tracker (“the App”) is a habit tracking application designed to help users build and maintain positive habits. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use the App.

By using Markk Habit Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Markk Habit Tracker allows you to create and manage habits. Habit-related data you enter into the App, such as habit names, schedules, and completion history, is stored locally on your device by default.

The App offers an optional cloud backup feature. If you choose to use this feature, your habit data may be securely stored in your personal cloud storage account:

  • Google Drive (for Android users)
  • iCloud (for iOS users)

To enable Google Drive backup on Android, you must sign in with your Google account and grant permission for the App to access your Drive storage for backup purposes. Authentication is handled securely by Google. The App does not access your Google account password or store your Google account credentials.

Cloud backup is optional and is only performed when you explicitly enable or trigger it within the App. Backup data is stored in your own cloud account.

We do not require account registration and do not directly collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Precise location

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect limited, non-personal information automatically, including:

  • App usage data (analytics)
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Device information (such as operating system version)

This information is collected in anonymized or aggregated form and is used solely to improve app stability, performance, and user experience.

1.3 Purchase and Subscription Information

Markk Habit Tracker offers optional in-app purchases and subscriptions. Purchase-related information is processed by third-party services and app stores. We do not have access to your full payment details.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain core app functionality
  • Enable optional cloud backup and restore features
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Manage subscriptions and purchase entitlements

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

Markk Habit Tracker uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • RevenueCat – for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Google Analytics – for collecting anonymized usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics – for collecting crash reports and diagnostics
  • Google Drive API – for optional Android cloud backup and restore
  • Apple iCloud – for optional iOS cloud backup and restore
  • Google Play Store – for Android purchases and distribution
  • Apple App Store – for iOS purchases and distribution

Cloud storage services operate under your personal account and are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Other third-party libraries used in the App are limited to user interface, visualization, and core functionality purposes and do not independently collect personal data.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Habit data is stored locally on your device by default.
  • If cloud backup is enabled, data is stored in your personal Google Drive or iCloud account.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your information.
  • No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Cloud backups remain fully under your control. Only you, as the account holder, can initiate deletion of your backup data.

You may permanently delete your cloud backup at any time by using the delete backup option provided within the App.

The developer does not have access to your cloud storage account and cannot access, modify, or delete your backup data.

If you uninstall the App, locally stored data will be removed from your device.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data directly within the App
  • Delete your local data by uninstalling the App
  • Delete cloud backup data at any time using the in-app delete option
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through your app store account

Markk Habit Tracker does not maintain user accounts or store personal habit data on servers controlled by the developer.

6. Children’s Privacy

Markk Habit Tracker does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
